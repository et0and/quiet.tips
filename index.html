Some quiet tips is an online resource designed to help those looking for some quiet time-out in their lives, if only for a brief moment. The design follows a simple chat interface, allowing for information to unfold as a "conversation" thread that can be looked through one piece at a time (inspired much by this great article by Benjamin Keyser)

Designed and written by Tom Hackshaw, and based on the excellent original work of Stephen Kirster's codebase

Need some help?
If you are in need of urgent help and feel as though you are likely to harm yourself or others, call 111 immediately

Otherwise, the following people are fantastic and here to help!

Youthline are here to help, and are available to freecall at 0800 376 633 or email talk@youthline.co.nz

The good people at Rainbow Youth are available for support to queer and gender diverse communities. Please refer to this page to get started.

For teenagers, the people at What's Up are here to help! Give them a call at 0800 942 8787 or chat to them online!
