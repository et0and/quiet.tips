One Size Fits Nose questions the nature of screen-based interaction paradigms. It calls for a world where interfaces transcend skeumorphism and representation. Intelligent, data-driven, mutable interfaces can respond to the context of reality and better meet the real goals of real people.

Designed, developed & written by Stephen Kistner
