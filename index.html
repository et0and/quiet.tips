Some quiet tips is an online resource designed to help those looking for some quiet time-out in their lives, if only for a brief moment. The design follows a simple chat interface, allowing for information to unfold as a "conversation" thread that can be looked through one piece at a time (inspired much by this great article by Benjamin Keyser)

Designed and written by Tom Hackshaw, and based on the excellent original work of Stephen Kirster's codebase
